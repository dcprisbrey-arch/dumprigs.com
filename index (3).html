About DumpRigs

Built on Grit & Experience

DumpRigs was built from the ground up with one goal: be the most reliable construction hauling operation in Utah. With nearly a decade hauling dirt, rock, aggregate, and everything in between — we know what contractors need and we deliver.

Beyond hauling, we operate a factoring division to help trucking companies get paid faster and manage cash flow — because we know how hard it is to keep a fleet moving when invoices sit for 30–60 days.

Our fleet of 69+ specialized trucks and trailers means we have the right equipment for any job, any size. We run 7 days a week, including weekends, because construction doesn't stop on Saturday.
